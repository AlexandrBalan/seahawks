2025 Roster

This is a simple look at a few key spots on the team.

Quarterback

Runs the offense and makes the throws.

  • Reads the defense
  • Leads the huddle

Running Back

Handles the ground game and short passes.

  • Quick cuts
  • Good balance

Wide Receiver

Gets open and makes catches downfield.

  • Speed
  • Strong hands

Linebacker

Helps stop the run and cover short passes.

  • Hard hits
  • Good awareness

Team Work Makes The Dream Work

The team is built from a bunch of different groups: offense, defense, and more. They all matter if you want to win.
